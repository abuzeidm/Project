Why Watch Pro Wrestling If You Know It's Fake?

For as long as I have been a fan of professional wrestling I have been asked the same question over and over and over again, and that question is, "why do you watch wrestling? You know it's fake right?"
Of course I know it's fake, but does that make it any less entertaining? No.

Pro Wrestling Terms

Kayfabe

Kayfabe is the portrayal of staged events within the industry as "real" or "true", specifically the portrayal of competition, rivalries, and relationships between participants as being genuine and not of a staged or predetermined nature of any kind.

Heel

A heel (also known as a rudo in lucha libre) is a wrestler who portrays a villain or a "bad guy" and acts as an antagonist.

Face

A face (babyface) is a heroic or a "good guy" wrestler, booked (scripted) by the promotion with the aim of being cheered by fans.

Smark

A fan of professional wrestling who is aware that the matches are scripted but enjoys them nonetheless.

WWE

World Wrestling Entertainment, Inc., WWE, is an American integrated media and entertainment company that is primarily known for the sport of professional wrestling. WWE has also branched out into other fields, including movies, football, and various other business ventures.

AEW

All Elite Wrestling, LLC is an American professional wrestling promotion founded in 2019. Its inception was announced by entrepreneurs Shahid Khan and his son Tony, with the former acting as the promotion's lead investor, and the latter being the founder, president and CEO of the company.

Brock Lesnar

Brock Edward Lesnar is an American professional wrestler, and former mixed martial artist and professional football player currently signed to WWE, where he performs on its Raw brand and is the current WWE Champion in his fifth reign.

The Rock

Dwayne Douglas Johnson, also known by his ring name, The Rock, is an American-Canadian actor, producer, and retired professional wrestler. Johnson was a professional wrestler for the World Wrestling Federation for eight years prior to pursuing an acting career.

About Pro Wrestling

Professional wrestling is a form of performance art and entertainment that combines athletics with theatrical performance. It takes the form of events, held by touring companies, that mimic a title-match combat sport.
